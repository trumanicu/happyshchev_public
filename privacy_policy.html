Privacy Policy

Thank you for using happyShchev. We take your privacy and data security very seriously.

Data Collection and Use

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or uploaded by this app.
  • All data (such as pet information, food records, poop records, etc.) is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not share your data with any third parties.

Permissions

  • Notification permission: Used for health and record reminders.
  • Photo library permission: Only used for customizing pet avatars. Your photos are never uploaded or shared.

Data Export and Deletion

  • You can export all your data at any time using the in-app export feature.
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data.

Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact us at happyshchev@gmail.com.
