Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how happyShchev (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles your information when you use our iOS application (“the App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding the data we collect, use, and store.

1. Information We Collect

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • All data, including pet profiles, food records, poop records, and user preferences, is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded or shared externally.

2. Use of Permissions

  • Notifications: The App requests notification permission solely to send you reminders about pet health, feeding, or other user-scheduled events. No notification data is shared or used for analytics.
  • Photo Library: The App may request access to your photo library only if you choose to set a custom pet avatar. Selected images are used exclusively within the App and are never uploaded, shared, or processed outside your device.

3. Data Security

  • All user data is stored securely on your device using Apple’s standard data protection mechanisms (such as CoreData and local storage).
  • We do not transmit, process, or store any user data on external servers.
  • It is your responsibility to keep your device secure and to use device-level security features (such as passcodes or Face ID).

4. Data Export and Deletion

  • You may export your data at any time using the App’s built-in export feature. Exported data is saved locally and is not transmitted to us.
  • Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all data stored on your device. We do not retain any backup or copy of your data.

5. Children’s Privacy

  • The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit any personal information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

  • The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  • No data is shared with third parties for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our official website. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
